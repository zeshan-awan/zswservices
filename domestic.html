Domestic Logistics

Our extensive domestic operations make ZWS a leading name in Pakistan’s courier and logistics industry. Through our Express Centres located across the country, we serve the largest customer base in Pakistan, providing a wide range of cost-effective transport and logistics solutions. These include domestic and international shipments of documents and parcels, document attestation, visa dropbox services, branchless banking, e-commerce fulfillment, and more. With round-the-clock operations, dedicated retail teams, the largest delivery fleet in Pakistan, and uncompromising service standards, ZWS ensures peace of mind for both individual and business customers. Our commitment to efficiency and reliability continues to set the benchmark for courier, transport, and logistics services in Pakistan.

Same Day Delivery

With a fleet of over 4,500 delivery vehicles and a dedicated air freighter connecting Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad, ZWS provides same-day document delivery within selected areas of major Pakistani cities.

Designed especially for business-to-business clients, our Same Day Delivery service ensures urgent shipments reach their destination quickly and securely. Empowered by strategically located delivery hubs in Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad, ZWS guarantees comprehensive coverage across these regions. Currently, our same-day delivery service is available in 11 key cities of Pakistan, making ZWS the preferred choice for fast, reliable, and professional courier and logistics services.

2nd Day Delivery

ZWS Economy Express is a dependable overland courier and logistics service offering economical shipping solutions across Pakistan. As a standard second-day delivery option, over 80% of Economy Express shipments are delivered in half the committed time, making it a fast, reliable, and budget-friendly choice for both individual and business customers.

Time Choice Delivery

ZSW Time Choice is a smart delivery fulfillment solution that allows customers to choose their preferred delivery date and time with complete flexibility. Shipments are delivered within a tight one-hour window, ensuring arrival no later than 30 minutes after the selected time slot for maximum convenience.

By combining the Time Choice service with their preferred shipping method, customers can manage their daily schedules without waiting all day for a critical delivery. This precision-based delivery service is ideal for businesses and individuals who value reliable, on-time logistics solutions. Delivery time commitments may vary based on the destination covered within the ZSW logistics network.

Black Box

The BlackBox Service is a cost-effective parcel delivery solution tailored for shipments weighing 1, 2, 3, 5, 10, 15, 20, and 25 kilograms, offering highly competitive flat rates. This service is ideal for sending a wide range of non-perishable goods, including fragile items, with safe and reliable handling.

With BlackBox, you can ship parcels to all major domestic locations, as well as selected international destinations, making it a dependable choice for both personal and business deliveries.

Designed for convenience and affordability, BlackBox ensures secure transport without compromising on speed or service quality.

Economy Express

ZSW Economy Express is a reliable second-day delivery service designed for cost-efficient shipping. More than 80% of shipments are delivered in significantly less time than the committed schedule, making it a trusted option for time-sensitive yet economical deliveries.

This service is available in ZSW-branded packaging with standard weight options of 5, 10, 15, 20, and 25 kilograms, while also supporting shipments of any weight up to 999 kg. Consignments exceeding 30 kg must be packed using customer-provided packaging. Please note that the Time Choice delivery option is not applicable with ZSW Economy Express.

Students Express

ZSW Students Express is a dependable and budget-friendly courier service designed specifically for students who need to send academic documents both locally and internationally. This document-only shipping solution ensures the safe and timely delivery of essential paperwork such as admission letters, educational transcripts, certificates, and official correspondence.

Ideal for prospective students and alumni, ZSW Students Express enables hassle-free document dispatch to and from educational institutions worldwide. With a simple flat-rate pricing structure, students can send important documents anywhere in the world with confidence, affordability, and complete peace of mind.
